LEPL Ilia State University GPA Calculation Rule

  1. GPA (Grade Point Average) represents the quotient of the sum of the products of GP accumulated in each course and the credits, divided by the total sum of credits:
    GPA = ∑ (GP × Credits) / ∑ Credits
  2. GP (Grade Point) represents the converted evaluation score on a 4-point scale.
  3. Evaluation categories:
    • F: 0-50 points (Fail, 0 GP)
    • E: 51-60 points (1.00 GP invariant)
    • D: 61-70 points
    • C: 71-80 points
    • B: 81-90 points
    • A: 91-95 points
    • A+: 96-100 points (4.00 GP invariant)
  4. According to the system, categories D, C, B, A (61-95 points) increase evenly and incrementally by 1/12 (~0.0833) point per score step over 60 points.
  5. GPA is issued for all university programs except the doctoral level.
  6. Only the final cumulative GPA is issued, reflected in the diploma supplement.
  7. When calculating the final GPA, fractions are rounded to the accuracy of tenths.
